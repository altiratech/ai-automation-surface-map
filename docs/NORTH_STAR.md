# AI Automation Surface Map North Star

Status: long-term ambition document

## Product Ambition

Build a reusable internal engine for choosing the right automation wedge across regulated workflows.

## Why This Could Matter

- product drift is expensive
- task-level clarity can make other product bets materially better
- this could become the hidden strategy engine behind multiple build tracks

## Long-Term Product Shape

- Future workflow layers:
  - more workflows
  - compare views
  - recommended agent patterns

- Future outputs:
  - ranked automation map
  - assist-versus-automate guidance
  - workflow-level playbooks

## Expansion Logic

- Natural expansion: support multiple regulated domains
- Dangerous expansion: generic workflow software
- What must remain true: the output has to drive real build decisions

## Strategic Positioning

- Eventual category: internal product-strategy engine
- Should not become: generic consulting deck generator
- Defensible if: it materially improves what gets built and what stays human
